History

Weithaga Boys High School is a fully Boarding A.C.K. Sponsored public secondary school. It is located in Weithaga location, Wangu Division of Kahuro District in Murang'a county. It was started in 1965 as a Day Harambee Secondary School to cater for the surrounding primary schools. Later, it was to serve the whole district as a fully boarding boys school. From an initial enrolment of only 37 students at its inception, The school has grouwn to a population of 1149 students currently.

The teaching staff currently 39 T.S.C and 6 B.O.M teachers. Over the years, The parents, local community and friends of the school have been pulling resources together to cater for the ever-growing population of students. Due to the free primary and population growth in our country, the transition rate from primary to secondary has gone up. The school catchment area is high.
